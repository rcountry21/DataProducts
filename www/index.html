MLB Wins vs Salary

Exploring team salaries and results

This app is a basic comparison tool of salaries in Major League Baseball and how it correlates to wins.
Data for this project was taken from Kaggle.com's History of Baseball dataset.

Notes on the dataset

  • 1985 was the first year salary information was collected for this data, so that is the first year of results displayed
  • Some teams have changed names. The most recent name is what appears in the dropdown. However, salary and win totals from previous franchise names are included
  • Some teams did not exist all the way back to 1985, their information is blank for years prior to existance

Getting Started

To use the app, select a team from the dropdown list. Each tab will display a different information as follows:

  • Wins - Win totals for each team for each year
  • Salary - A bar for the amount the team spent in salary for each year, average MLB salary for that year is the other bar
  • $ per win - A bar for the amount the team spent in salary per win, average MLB salary per win for that year is the other bar
